Rent my car: Development Version

Rent my car is a p2p car rental marketplace where car owners can make their cars available for others to rent for specified periods of time. All rental details are managed by Rent My Car while car preparation for a renter is handled by the car owner. Rent a car handles vetting car renters and car owners and the rental transaction.

Car Owner

Janet is traveling out of town for a month on a business assignment oveseas. During her trip her car will sit in her garage while she pays her car payments, insurance and garage rental fees. Alternatively, Janet can put her car on the Rent My Car market and make money that can go towards maintaining her car while she is out of town.

Car Renter

Tom has been assigned to a project in Chicago for a month where he will need a means for commuting between several sites for the project. He would like to find an affordable car rental for the duration of his stay. Tom has a very good driving record with no accidents or tickets in the last year and plans to drive the car for no more than 50 miles per day.

FAQ

Who can list their car for rental?
Any car owner can list their car for rental so long as they meet the following criteria: (1) Car is in good condition to drive, (2) Car is available as published by the car owner, (3) Car owner registers with Rent My Car ( See car owner registration requirements in FAQ ).
Who can rent a car?
Anyone registered with Rent My Car can rent a car listed on the marketplace. See car renter registration requirements in FAQ.
How do I get paid for renting my car?
All payments are handled by Rent My Car. Renters pay Rent My Car directly and once the transaction begins Rent My Car will hold payment plus any required deposit until the car is returned to the renter. At that point payment is deposited into the renters account less Rent My Car's commission. Renters acknowledge they have received the car in the stated condition and car owners also acknowledge they receive their cars back in the expected condition for payments to be processed normally. If any exceptions are raised by either party Rent My Car will address them based on the type of exception. See various exceptions and how they are handled in later FAQ questions.
